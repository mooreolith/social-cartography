To enter yourself, click the text box with the placeholder "Person@Group", and enter your name, "First Middle@Last", without the quotation marks. Three vertices will appear. One for your person, one for your group, and one for your role in that group. Repeat this process up your foodchain. Be specific: First Middle@Department, Department@Division, Division@Company, Company@Owner, etc. This will provide you with a visual overview of your social network. Also, former names connect, mostly women, to disparate groups of people she might have shared that name with. For more information, please email Joshua Moore.
